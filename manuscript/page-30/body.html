Given that Sun is only ~4.57 billion years old it has been around the GC only 20 times so far. The time period to go around the galaxy is also called a Galactic Year. Galactic Year(Gyr) is a convenient unit of time used to describe cosmic events in astronomy. For example, life on Earth began only 15 Gyrs ago, Big bang happened 61 Gyrs ago and so on.
