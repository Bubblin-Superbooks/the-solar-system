Earth, our home, is the third and densest of planets in the Solar System. It is a terrestrial planet, meaning it has a solid planetary surface. Earth is made up of mostly iron and other heavier elements of the periodic table. About 71% of Earth's surface is covered in water, with oceans, seas, polar ice-caps etc., followed by a thin veil of atmospheric air that surrounds it and protects us from Solar Radiation, Cosmic Rays and falling objects like meteorites.
